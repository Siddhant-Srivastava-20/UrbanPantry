Help & Support

How can we assist you today?

1. How do I place an order?

Browse through the menu, add items to your cart, and complete the order by selecting your address and payment method.

2. How can I track my order?

You can track your order in real-time from the 'My Orders' section after logging in.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, Wallets, Cash on Delivery, Debit/Credit Cards, and Net Banking.

4. Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 1–2 minutes after placing them. Modification depends on restaurant approval.

5. I received the wrong or damaged item. What should I do?

You can report the issue in the 'Help' section of the My Orders page. Refunds or replacements will be processed quickly.

6. My payment failed. Will I get a refund?

If the amount was deducted, it will automatically be refunded within 2–5 working days.

7. How do I apply coupon codes?

You can enter the coupon code on the checkout page before completing payment.

8. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us through the in-app chat support or email support@urbanpantry.com.

9. What is the average delivery time?

Delivery time varies between 20–45 minutes depending on restaurant distance and order volume.

10. Why is a restaurant unavailable?

Restaurants may be unavailable due to high demand, maintenance, or being outside your delivery zone.

11. How do I update my delivery address?

You can add or update your address from the Profile → Manage Addresses section.

12. Where can I see my previous orders?

Your complete order history can be viewed in the 'My Orders' section.

13. How do I delete or deactivate my account?

You can request account deletion through the Help → Account section. Processing may take up to 48 hours.

14. Are my orders cooked fresh?

Yes! All meals on UrbanPantry are prepared fresh after your order is placed. No pre-made food is served.

15. Why didn’t I receive my offer or cashback?

Some offers are limited to specific payment methods or minimum order values. Check the offer terms for details.

16. Does UrbanPantry offer membership or subscription?

Yes! UrbanPass offers free delivery, exclusive discounts, and early access to special dishes.

17. What safety and hygiene measures do you follow?

All partner kitchens follow FSSAI guidelines, sanitized kitchens, and sealed packaging protocols.

18. Can I place multiple orders at once?

Yes, but each order will have its own delivery time and charges.

19. The app/website is not loading. What should I do?

Try refreshing the page, switching networks, or clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, contact support.
